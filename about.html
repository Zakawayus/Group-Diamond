About Us

At Life Awareness Center (LAC) we are focused on eradicating the rising level of ignorance and negligence of health related issues in the society. The essence of LAC is to keep the public informed and updated on health issues and also educated them on the importance of healthy living.

Our mission states that AWARENESS IS LIFE and so we are well equip with a team of health and safety professionals, research and tech savvy experts and managers who provide general information and daily tips to help our viewer. We are also in collaboration with top health agencies who also provide financial assistance and content verification. We provide nothing but the best at LAC.
