Frequently Asked Questions

Is the information provided confidential?

Yes. We apply strict standard for privacy and confidentiality

Is there a time limit for completing my registration?

Yes. The Life Awareness Center (LAC) process is time sensitive, so you will want to act fast! The form is eazy and simple to fill. For clarification, contact our Support Center in About Page

Upon completion of registration, am I eligible for insurance coverage?

For now, Life Awareness Center (LAC) is yet to cater for life insurance coverage. Our subscriber would be updated as soon as it is available.

What happen after my Registration?

Life Awareness Center (LAC) will review the form and generate response within ten working days. After receiving the information, the response will either notify you of the final determination regarding your subscription or request additional information.

Can I modify the information on my Registration Form after submission?

If there is an update or information you would want to change or add, you can do that on the REQUEST INFO below

How many times can I register?

Just once is enough

What are the working duration of LAC Support Center?

LAC Support Center will be available from 8am-5pm (WAT) on week days
